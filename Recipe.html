Recipe For Chicken Karahi:

Ingredients:

  • Chicken
  • Tomatoes
  • Ginger
  • Chilli
  • Salt

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pan and fry chicken with ginger-garlic paste until golden
  2. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft and mushy
  3. Add salt, red chili powder, and green chilies, then cook for 10-15 minutes
  4. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with naan or roti
